Buyback & Burn

Rynix AI charges small fees on automation triggers, premium features, and agent job settlement. A portion of these fees is used to buy back $RYNIX and remove it from circulation permanently, creating deflationary pressure as protocol usage grows.

  • 20% of protocol revenue goes to the buyback and burn program.
  • 50% of buyback tokens are burned.
  • 50% of buyback tokens are distributed to stakers and active users.
  • Burn metrics are published on-chain and visible in the Rynix AI Terminal.
