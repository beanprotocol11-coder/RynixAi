Introduction

Crypto markets move 24/7. Data is fragmented, execution is complex, and the best opportunities often arrive when you are not watching. Rynix AI solves this by combining a large language model, live market data, and Robinhood Chain settlement into a single autonomous trading companion.

Traders describe what they want in plain English. Rynix AI interprets the intent, builds the strategy, monitors the triggers, and prepares execution — all settled transparently on Robinhood Chain.

Vision

Make sophisticated, on-chain trading as simple as speaking your mind. Rynix AI becomes the default interface for Robinhood Chain traders by fusing real-time data, AI reasoning, and trustless execution.

Mission

Deliver a platform that accepts market analysis, trade instructions, and automation rules through natural language. As the protocol grows, Rynix AI will expand into:

  • Cross-venue funding and liquidation monitoring
  • Whale wallet and exchange flow correlation
  • Portfolio-level risk management and auto-hedging
  • Agent-to-agent job markets on Robinhood Chain

Rynix AI Terminal

The Terminal is the command center for Rynix AI. It is publicly available and provides:

  • Natural-language trading — describe your trade in plain English.
  • 24/7 automation — define rules and let Rynix monitor them.
  • Market analysis — multi-timeframe indicators and price context.
  • Research & sentiment — web search and social signal aggregation.
  • Multi-platform access — web, Telegram, X, and agent networks.
  • Real-time context — live prices, wallet balance, and open positions.

How Automation Works

  1. User Intent — any natural-language description of conditions and actions.
  2. AI Understands — Rynix parses intent and selects the optimal trigger type.
  3. Smart Trigger — price, pattern, time, portfolio, or indicator trigger is set.
  4. Idle Monitor — lightweight background watcher waits for the trigger.
  5. Trigger Fires — Rynix evaluates the full context and prepares execution.
  6. Trade Settled — the user confirms and the order is settled on Robinhood Chain.

Technology & Architecture

Rynix AI runs on a modular, scalable architecture:

  1. Data Sources — user input, market data (CoinGecko), wallet snapshots, automation history.
  2. Context Builder — assembles a unified prompt with system instructions, user profile, portfolio snapshot, and conversation history.
  3. Agent Core — understands intent, decides on tools, and plans actions.
  4. Actions — price queries, strategy creation, order preparation, and status checks.
  5. User Receives — streaming response, real-time updates, and confirmation status.
